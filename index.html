putu ayu cake

Putu ayu cake is a market snack made with flour as the main ingredient. This cake is made by steaming and served with grated coconut. Usually the Putu Ayu made by Hegarmukti residents is green and white with a combination of savory and sweet flavors that is very appetizing. The name putu cake apparently comes from an abbreviation, namely putu, which means "Steam Powered Money Seeker". The name is taken from the seller's habit, where they prepare putu cake by steaming it over steam or hot steam.
